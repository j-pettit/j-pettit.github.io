A Little About Myself

Hi, I'm Julian.

I'm a 3rd year Mechatronics Engineering student at the University of Waterloo. I like developing code with a cup of coffee early in the morning, prototyping control circuits and creating my own PCBs, and learning about the world around me.

As an Engineer I hope to improve the world for everyone who lives in it by applying my knowledge and skillset to the problems people face every day. I am interested in energy production and consumption, aerospace, prosthetics, and robotics.

If you are interested in me or what I do, please take a look at my Resume and Github, or feel free to contact me directly.
