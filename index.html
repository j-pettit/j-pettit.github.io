Welcome.

Hello. Welcome to my small piece of the web. The aim of this website is not necessarily to demonstrate my skills or showcase my ability, but perhaps to connect us as two people living our lives as best as we can. Please peruse this page at your leisure, and feel free to jump between sections or skip them as you see fit.

My network includes other online resources and presences, which have been handily provided for you below. These should give you a modest understanding of my professional and academic circles and achievements, although I prefer to make these impressions in person. Please take special note of my resume, as this website is not meant to be a substitute for it.

You.

You aren't like other people. You're different. Unique. Your entire life has been a learning experience, and each trial that you've surmounted has left a profound mark on your life.

When you were young, you dreamed big. Nothing could hold you back. Through your formative years, you developped strong friendships and joined in committed relationships. Maybe you aced school, maybe you struggled. Either way, you learned that the real value of education is a capacity to learn and a clear mind.

At some point in your life you realized that life won't eventually start to make sense. Few people have a singular life-changing experience, but rather grow and change through constant mutation. You understand that discipline is a much more powerful force than motivation, and will support you for as long as you cultivate it.

You sound like my kind of person.
