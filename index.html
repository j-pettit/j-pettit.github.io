Hi, I'm Julian. I'm a 19 year old Canadian student.

I just finished a four month internship at Innovasium Digital as a web application developer and am currently beginning my second year at the University of Waterloo, studying mechatronics engineering and muddy parking lots.

Right now I'm having a lot of fun playing around with arduinos and raspberry pis. I completed a DIY emulator tutorial, and once the glow of playing it with my friends wore off I got my hands on a discount arduino and started a thermostat project loosely based on this guide. I will update with results as soon as possible! I hope to eventually build this project into a home-automation system for my apartment.

I speak English, French, and German fluently, and I'm learning beginner's Russian in my free time. I enjoy cycling, kendo, drama TV series, and playing the ukulele.

I'm currently looking for a winter co-op position, between the months of January and April, to fulfill one of my school's educational requirements. My education and career interests span the fields of electronics, software development, mechanics, system design, and automation. Please get in touch with me using one of the methods detailed below.

I like to keep an optimistic perspective on the future. I hope to graduate with a wealth of unique and interesting co-op experiences, and find a niche in some mechatronics field that appeals to me. Maybe space exploration, or biomechanics, or medicine.
