The question

What does it mean to be human? A quick google search gives the rather unsatisfying answer of

a human being, especially a person as distinguished from an animal or (in science fiction) an alien.
This is all very well and good, but really only shifts the burden of definition to person. Let us delve further. Unfortunately, this was destined to be a splash into a pond, rather than a graceful leap into a sea of mystery and understanding with the defenition for person
a human being regarded as an individual
really just brings us back to where we started. I am philosopher by trade or education, but I am a thinker, and as such I believe I have the right to explore the question in a less literal meaning. That being said, rather then dwell on definitions, please join me in the exploration of my humanity, and the driving force behind my actions.
